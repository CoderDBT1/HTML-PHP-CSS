About Persona Fest

Persona Fest is the annual techno-cultural festival of the MIT ADT University Pune. It is a three-day long University Fest, which involves in all types of entertaining events. is truly a cultural extravaganza which is getting humongous participation and overwhelming response from the students across the length and breadth of Maharashtra. More than 11,000 Students from the 125+ Institutions and Universities participate in this MIT-ADT University Flagship event.

About Persona Fashion Show

In this event participants are expected to show off their fashion skills by taking up either of the three themes, namely Colors of India, Retro or Electro. This not only showcases the fashionable talent of the students but also boosts their mentality to a different level in terms of extracurricular activities.

About Vishwanath Sports Meet

Vishwanath Sports meet is the annual sports event of MIT ADT University; it is a gala event in which more than 4000 students from all over Maharashtra participate in various games to prove their metal as an athlete. ... All in all this event wishes to bring various young athletes to compete in a healthy manner.

About Sangeet Kala

MIT Vishwashanti Sangeet Kala Academy (MIT VSKA) is a truly unique academic institute that aims at reviving Indian Classical Music and Dance in its purest forms. They channelize their efforts to project Indian Culture, Traditions and Philosophy in their truest form on the global map. This event is aimed to bring out the best of best skills of the respective students and alumni.
